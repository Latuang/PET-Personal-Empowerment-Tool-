Privacy Policy for PET (Personal Empowerment Tool)

Last updated: September 1, 2025

PET (Personal Empowerment Tool) is a Chrome extension designed to motivate and support users while they browse, study, or work. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the extension handles your data.

What Data We Collect

PET does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or browsing history.

The extension only stores the motivational lines you provide, and these are saved locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device or gets sent to external servers.

Permissions Explained

  • Storage: Saves your custom pep lines locally so PET can display them later.
  • Tabs: Allows PET to show your motivational companion across different browser tabs.
  • Alarms: Used to schedule periodic nudges.

Third-Party Services

PET does not use or connect to any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or remote code sources. All functionality runs locally on your browser.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share any user data to third parties.
